Our Farm

We are a small family owned farm in Graham, WA. We started raising our own birds for a healthy resourse for eggs and meat. Our flock has a large coop with unrestricted access to a fully fenced yard. Our flock is a mixed group of several species of Chickens, Ducks and Geese.
